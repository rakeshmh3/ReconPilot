# ReconPilot

**ReconPilot** automates balance‑sheet reconciliation by reading workbooks, analyzing differences, computing ageing, assessing risk, and generating actionable insights. It streamlines financial close, enhances audit readiness, and delivers fast, standardized, enterprise‑grade reconciliation intelligence.
________________________________________
**Project Overview**
ReconPilot AI is an enterprise-grade Finance AI Agent designed to automate Balance Sheet Account Reconciliation analysis during the financial close process. The solution intelligently reads reconciliation workbooks, extracts sign-off information, analyzes reconciling items, calculates ageing, assesses financial risk, and generates actionable recommendations. By combining document understanding with AI-driven reasoning, the agent transforms manual reconciliation reviews into a faster, standardized, and auditable process while providing management with consolidated visibility across all balance sheet accounts.
________________________________________
**Problem Statement**
Finance teams typically maintain separate reconciliation workbooks for every balance sheet account, resulting in hundreds or even thousands of files that must be manually reviewed each reporting period. Controllers and reviewers spend significant effort consolidating reconciliation data, verifying differences between SAP and subledger balances, identifying aged reconciling items, and determining whether outstanding differences pose material financial reporting risks. This manual approach is time-consuming, inconsistent, difficult to scale, and increases the likelihood of delayed issue identification, audit observations, and extended financial close cycles.
________________________________________
**Solution Statement**
ReconPilot AI addresses these challenges through specialized AI agents for document understanding, reconciliation analysis, ageing computation, risk assessment, and recommendation generation. 
________________________________________
**Conclusion**
ReconPilot AI modernizes the balance sheet reconciliation process by combining intelligent document processing with accounting expertise and autonomous decision support. Instead of simply extracting data, the agent interprets reconciliation differences, highlights ageing trends, prioritizes high-risk accounts, and recommends appropriate resolution actions. This enables finance organizations to shift from reactive manual reviews toward proactive financial risk management while improving consistency, transparency, and operational efficiency throughout the month-end close process.
________________________________________

 **Value Statement**
ReconPilot AI delivers measurable business value by significantly reducing reconciliation review effort, accelerating financial close timelines, improving audit readiness, enhancing visibility into aged reconciling items, and enabling early identification of material financial reporting risks. By providing standardized analysis, explainable risk assessments, and actionable recommendations across every balance sheet account, the solution empowers finance leaders to make faster, data-driven decisions while strengthening internal controls and governance. The result is a more efficient, scalable, and resilient finance operation that supports higher-quality financial reporting and improved business outcomes.
